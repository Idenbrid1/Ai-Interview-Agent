# Unit of Work 4: AI Interview System

## Overview

This unit handles the core AI avatar interview functionality, including the interview session interface, AI avatar interaction, and interview completion workflow.

## User Stories

### AI Interview Interface

- **039_Interview Session Page**: As a user, I want to conduct interviews with an AI avatar through voice conversation with real-time transcription, status display, timer, and basic interview information displayed
- **040_Interview Completion Page**: As a user, I want to see a completion confirmation after finishing an interview with information about where to find my report

### Avatar Settings

- **060_Avatar Voice Settings**: As a user, I want to select from multiple voice options (Voice 1, Voice 2, Voice 3) for the AI avatar to personalize my interview experience

### Meeting AI Avatar (Top Page)

- **Meeting AI Avatar**: As a user, I want to access the AI avatar interview functionality directly from the top page with theme switching between job hunting and career change modes

## Acceptance Criteria

### AI Interview Functionality

- Users can conduct voice-based interviews with AI avatar
- Real-time speech-to-text transcription is displayed during interviews
- Interview status is clearly shown (preparing, in progress, completed)
- Timer displays remaining or elapsed time
- Basic interview information is visible (schedule, start time, user name, company name)
- Users can complete interviews with a dedicated button
- Camera on/off toggle is available with one-tap switching
- Microphone controls are available
- Interview recording capability is implemented

### Avatar Customization

- Users can select from multiple voice options for the AI avatar
- Voice settings are saved and applied to future interviews
- Voice changes take effect immediately

### User Experience

- Interview interface serves as the main top page
- Theme switching between job hunting and career change is available
- Interface is optimized for seamless interview experience
- All interview settings are consolidated in the settings page
